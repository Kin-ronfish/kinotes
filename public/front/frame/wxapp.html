  • 页面wxml

标签内和标签外引用变量都需要加上{{}}

条件判断：wx:if、wx:elif、wx:else

<block wx:if="{{flag===1}}">
  <view>xx</view>
</block>
<block wx:elif="{{flag===2}}">
  <view>xx</view>
</block>
<block wx:else>
  <view>xx</view>
</block>

循环渲染：wx:for、wx:for-item、wx:key

<block wx:for="{{list}}" wx:for-item="item" wx:key="index">
  <text>{{index + 1}}. {{item.name}}</text>
</block>

绑定点击事件：bindtap="handleEvent"

  • 页面js

  data: {
